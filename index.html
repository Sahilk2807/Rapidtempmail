Frequently Asked Questions

Are the inboxes private?

No. All temporary email addresses are public and have no password. Anyone who knows the address can see the inbox. Do not use it for sensitive information.

How long are emails stored?

Emails are automatically deleted after a few hours to ensure privacy and save space. This is a temporary service, not a permanent inbox.

Can I send emails?

No, this service is for receiving emails only. You cannot send replies or compose new emails from the temporary address.
